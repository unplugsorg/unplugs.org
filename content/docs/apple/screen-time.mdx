---
title: "Screen Time"
---

This tutorial will explain every Screen Time setting.  
Open Settings and click on Screen Time.

## App & Website Activity

Enable App & Website Activity to access App & Website Activity, Downtime, App Limits, and Always Allowed.

## Downtime

App & Website Activity must be turned on to access Downtime.

<img
  src="/apple/screen-time/8.jpeg"
  alt="Downtime 1"
  style={{ width: '50%' }}
/>

Enable Scheduled  
You can set a schedule for every day or days of the week.  

<img
  src="/apple/screen-time/9.jpeg"
  alt="Downtime 2"
  style={{ width: '50%' }}
/>

Click Time to edit schedule.  
If you want downtime 24/7, set the schedule from 3:00 am to 2:59 am.  

<img
  src="/apple/screen-time/10.jpeg"
  alt="Downtime 3"
  style={{ width: '50%' }}
/>

If you want to block apps and websites during downtime, [set a screen time password](#lock-screen-time-settings).  
You can set always allowed apps during downtime, but you cannot allow all websites in Safari during downtime.

## App Limits

App & Website Activity must be turned on to access App Limits  

<img
  src="/apple/screen-time/12.jpeg"
  alt="App Limits 1"
  style={{ width: '50%' }}
/>

Click Add Limit.  

<img
  src="/apple/screen-time/13.jpeg"
  alt="App Limits 2"
  style={{ width: '50%' }}
/>

Choose app categories, individual apps, and websites to set a limit on. Click Next.  

<img
  src="/apple/screen-time/14.jpeg"
  alt="App Limits 3"
  style={{ width: '50%' }}
/>

You can set a time limit for every day or selected days.  
You cannot set a time limit of zero; instead, limit it to 1 minute. Click Add.  

<img
  src="/apple/screen-time/15.jpeg"
  alt="App Limits 4"
  style={{ width: '50%' }}
/>

If you want to block apps and websites after the time limit is reached, [set a screen time password](#lock-screen-time-settings).

## Always Allowed

App & Website Activity must be turned on to access Always Allowed  

<img
  src="/apple/screen-time/18.jpeg"
  alt="Always Allowed"
  style={{ width: '50%' }}
/>

You can allow apps during downtime and those included in an app limit.

## Screen Distance

<img
  src="/apple/screen-time/26.jpeg"
  alt="Screen Distance"
  style={{ width: '50%' }}
/>

Enable Screen Distance to get a popup to move your iPhone or iPad at least 1 foot away from your face when it is too close.

## Communication Safety

<img
  src="/apple/screen-time/35.jpeg"
  alt="Communication Safety"
  style={{ width: '50%' }}
/>

Enable Communication Safety to blur images and videos detected as inappropriate. Users can still unblur them.

## Content & Privacy Restrictions

Enable Content & Privacy Restrictions to access settings.

### App Installations & Purchases

<img
  src="/apple/screen-time/39.jpeg"
  alt="App Installations"
  style={{ width: '50%' }}
/>

Prevent installing new apps (including updates), disable in-app purchases, or require iCloud password.  
Prevent deleting apps.

### Allowed Apps & Features

<img
  src="/apple/screen-time/41.jpeg"
  alt="Allowed Apps"
  style={{ width: '50%' }}
/>

Disable built-in apps and features (e.g., Camera disables FaceTime).

### Store, Web, Siri & Game Center Content

#### Allowed Store Content

<img
  src="/apple/screen-time/43.jpeg"
  alt="Store Content"
  style={{ width: '50%' }}
/>

Change age rating of accessible content from the store.

#### Web Content

<img
  src="/apple/screen-time/45.jpeg"
  alt="Web Content"
  style={{ width: '50%' }}
/>

Click Limit Adult Websites to block or allow websites.  

<img
  src="/apple/screen-time/46.jpeg"
  alt="Web Content 2"
  style={{ width: '50%' }}
/>

To allow only certain websites, click Only Approved Websites.

#### Siri & Game Center

<img
  src="/apple/screen-time/47.jpeg"
  alt="Siri & Game Center"
  style={{ width: '50%' }}
/>

Disable Siri web search or explicit language.  
Disable screen recording or multiplayer content.

### Privacy & Allow Changes To

Set privacy settings for apps and prevent changes to device settings.

Enter a recovery Apple Account in case you forget your passcode.  
Store credentials in [Plucky Lockbox](https://lockbox.pluckeye.net/).  

<img

<img
